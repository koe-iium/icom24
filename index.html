About

The 9ᵗʰ International Conference on Mechatronics Engineering (ICOM’24) is a series of conference organized by the Kulliyyah of Engineering, International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) and Mechatronics Engineering Department, as a part of IIUM Engineering Congress (IEC) 2024. ICOM’24 focuses on bringing together researchers, experts and practitioners to address the latest trends and technologies in the multifaceted field of Mechatronics Engineering
