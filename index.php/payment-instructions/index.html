Payment Instructions

A) Via IIUM EzPay Payment (Local Participants)

  • Step 1

    Visit IIUM Payment Gateway at the following lnk

  • Step 2

    Go to Virtual Counter menu

  • Step 3

    Select Payment type > Conference > 9th ICOM 2024 [ Organized by : KOE date 13 – 14 August 2024 ]

  • Step 4

    Enter the payer's information and specify the correct payment amount

  • Step 5

    Select your payment method
    Types of payment accepted:

    i. FPX - Financial Process Exchange (Saving / Current / Credit Card)

    ii. E-Wallet (Boost / GrabPay / Touch n Go eWallet / ShopeePay)

    iii. Credit Card for International card issuer (currently under maintenance)

  • Step 6

    Complete the payment by following instructions on your screen.

  • Step 7

    Complete

B) Via Flywire (International Participants)

  • Step 1

    Visit Flywire Payment Gateway at https://landing-pages.flywire.com/landing/iium

  • Step 2

    Go to Conference Fee menu

  • Step 3

    Enter the payer's information and complete the transaction.
